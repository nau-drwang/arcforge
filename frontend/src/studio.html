Studio

A detail-driven fantasy sculpture studio.

ArcForge combines miniature craft, character art, scenic display design, and hand-painted surface detail to create fantasy works with strong visual presence.

Studio language

The work emphasizes expressive silhouettes, layered armor detail, dramatic bases, saturated color, and atmospheric storytelling. Each piece is photographed and organized as part of a growing digital artwork library.

Surface detailHand-painted finishes, metallic accents, weathering, and layered textures.
Story-driven formPoses, flags, weapons, terrain, and lighting effects used to create narrative tension.
International presentationEnglish-first artwork pages with Chinese support and future multilingual expansion.
