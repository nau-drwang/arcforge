Custom Commissions

Commission original fantasy sculptures and collector display pieces.

ArcForge works with collectors and creative clients to shape character sculptures, battle dioramas, and display-ready fantasy works around story, pose, mood, finish, and presentation.

Commission process

Each inquiry begins with the idea: character type, scale, visual references, color mood, and display environment. After the direction is clear, the studio can discuss feasibility, timeline, pricing, and media assets for the final work.

1. ConceptShare theme, character, references, size, and intended display setting.
2. Design directionAgree on mood, pose, material finish, base, and visual storytelling.
3. CreationThe piece is built, painted, detailed, photographed, and prepared for delivery or presentation.

Commission categories

ArcForge is best suited for high-detail fantasy figures, warrior and sorcerer characters, creature scenes, banners, terrain bases, and dramatic dioramas.
